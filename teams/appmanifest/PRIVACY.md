# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-17_

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Quill** Microsoft Teams application
("the App") handles information when you interact with it.

Quill is an **open-source, self-hosted** bot. The project maintainers do not
operate a centralized service and do not receive any of your data. The
organization or individual that deployed Quill in your tenant (the
"Operator") is the data controller for any information processed by their
instance of the App.

## 1. Information Processed

When you interact with the App, the following data is transmitted from
Microsoft Teams to the Operator's self-hosted instance so that the bot can
reply:

- **Message content** — the text, commands, or @mentions you send to the
  bot.
- **Attachments** — images, voice messages, audio, and files you upload in
  a conversation with the bot. Voice and audio attachments are transcribed
  to text via a speech-to-text provider (e.g., OpenAI Whisper) when the
  Operator enables that feature.
- **Identity metadata** — your Teams user ID, display name, conversation ID,
  channel ID, and tenant ID, provided by the Microsoft Bot Framework so the
  bot can route replies and enforce access controls.
- **Session state** — a short-lived mapping between your Teams conversation
  and an AI agent session maintained in memory by the Operator's instance.

## 2. How the Information Is Used

Processed data is used only to:

- deliver your message to the configured AI agent and stream the agent's
  response back to your Teams conversation;
- transcribe voice/audio input and, when enabled, synthesize voice output;
- enforce access-control rules (e.g., allowed user IDs, allowed channels)
  configured by the Operator;
- provide operational visibility (logs, session lists) to the Operator.

Message content and attachments may be transmitted to third-party AI
providers configured by the Operator (for example, the ACP agent backend,
OpenAI, or Google Gemini) strictly for the purpose of generating a
response. Their use of that data is governed by their own privacy policies.

## 3. Data Retention

- **Session state** is held in memory and evicted automatically when the
  session is idle, reset, or the bot process restarts.
- **Attachments** downloaded for processing are written to a temporary
  directory on the Operator's host and are intended for short-term use only.
- **Logs** generated by the Operator's instance may contain metadata about
  your interactions. Retention of those logs is determined by the Operator.

The project maintainers do not retain any user data because they do not
operate a hosted instance of the App.

## 4. Data Sharing

The App does not sell user data. Data may be shared with:

- third-party AI and speech providers selected and configured by the
  Operator, solely to fulfill a request;
- Microsoft, as required to deliver messages through the Bot Framework.

## 5. Security

The App relies on Microsoft Bot Framework authentication (JWT validation of
inbound requests and OAuth2 client credentials for outbound calls) to
protect traffic between Teams and the bot. Transport-layer security
(HTTPS/TLS) and the security of the self-hosted environment are the
responsibility of the Operator.

## 6. Your Choices

- Stop using the bot, remove it from a conversation, or uninstall the App
  to stop further data processing.
- Use the in-chat `/reset` command to clear the current session's in-memory
  state.
- Contact the Operator in your tenant for requests related to data access,
  correction, or deletion held in logs or backups under their control.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use in business and developer environments and is
not directed to children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will
be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

## 9. Contact

Questions about this policy can be filed as issues at
<https://github.com/neilkuan/quill/issues>. For data-subject requests
specific to your tenant's deployment, contact the Operator that installed
the App.
